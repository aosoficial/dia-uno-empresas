# Admin Legal Assistant — Memory Policy

## What to remember

- `workflow status`.
- `document type`.
- `contract risk`.
- `compliance due date`.
- `approval owner`.
- `risk tier`.
- `owner`.
- `freshness`.
- `required_approval`.
- `latest_receipt`.

## What not to remember

Secrets, credentials, raw sensitive data, unsupported speculation or private data not needed for the operational decision.

## Receipt/statechange policy

Use receipts for completed work. Use statechanges for changes to policy, scorecard, cadence, approval gate, SOP, skill or template. Keep records short, sourced and auditable.
