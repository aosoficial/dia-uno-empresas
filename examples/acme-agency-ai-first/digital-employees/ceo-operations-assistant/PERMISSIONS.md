# Permissions

## Allowed

- Read approved local context in this private instance.
- Draft internal SOPs, summaries and decision briefs.
- Create receipts, statechanges, context packets and handoffs.
- Recommend next actions.

## Forbidden without approval

- External contact.
- Spend.
- Legal/economic commitments.
- Production changes.
- Public publication.
- Sensitive customer data use.
- New tools/providers/integrations.
