# Operations Skills

## Delivery QA Review

Trigger: before a client-facing deliverable, weekly report or onboarding handoff leaves the team.

Steps:
1. Confirm the service promise and current delivery stage.
2. Check owner, source, target, required inputs and approval boundaries.
3. Compare the artifact against the QA checklist and definition of done.
4. Record missing evidence, blockers and proposed fix.
5. Leave a QA review receipt.

## Handoff Improvement

Trigger: when work stalls between sales, delivery, customer success or reporting.

Steps:
1. Name the handoff source and target.
2. Identify what information, asset or decision was missing.
3. Draft the smallest SOP/checklist change that prevents recurrence.
4. Ask for human approval if the change affects clients, scope, tools, economics or sensitive data.
5. Leave a handoff improvement receipt.
