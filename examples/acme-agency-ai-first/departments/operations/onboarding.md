# Operations Onboarding

## First 30 minutes

1. Read `department-brain.md` and confirm the current service promise.
2. Pick one synthetic or approved active delivery flow.
3. Map stages from sales handoff to first client-visible outcome.
4. Identify missing owner, missing input, missing QA or missing receipt.
5. Draft one internal improvement and save a receipt.

## Do not start with

- Contacting clients.
- Changing production tools.
- Importing real client data into public examples.
- Approving economic, legal or scope decisions.
