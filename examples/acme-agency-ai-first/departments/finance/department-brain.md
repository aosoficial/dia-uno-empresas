# Finance Brain

Department: `finance`
Mission: cash, invoicing, margins, unit economics, forecasting and spend review.
Human owner: `Example Operator`
Digital employee: `Finance Assistant`
Freshness: `draft until reviewed`

## Mission and business outcome

This brain turns cash, invoicing, margins, unit economics, forecasting and spend review into operational state with owners, evidence, approval boundaries and weekly review. It is not a replacement for professional accountability; it makes the work visible, auditable and safer to delegate to humans and digital employees.

Business outcome: clean cash visibility, margin discipline, safer spending decisions and earlier detection of financial risk.

## Operating questions

1. What is the current cash position and runway?
2. Which invoices are pending, overdue or blocked?
3. Which services are profitable after delivery cost?
4. Which spend needs approval before commitment?
5. What forecast changed this week and why?
6. Which financial risk must be escalated?

## Weekly cadence

- Review scorecard and blockers.
- Confirm owner for every open commitment.
- Check stale decisions and missing evidence.
- Convert one repeated issue into a SOP, skill or template improvement.
- Create a weekly department receipt.
- Escalate unresolved approval-boundary issues to Dirección.

## Required receipts

Create a receipt when a decision, review, handoff, risk, approval, rejected action or material update occurs. Each receipt must include what changed, why, source/provenance, owner, freshness, allowed action, forbidden action, required approval and evidence.

## Context packet handoff

When this department needs another department, prepare a context packet with source, summary, owner, assumptions, risks, required decision, approval boundary, expected outcome and receipt link. Do not rely on memory from chat alone.

## Skills

See `skills.md` in this department pack. Core skills should create review drafts, risk summaries, SOPs, scorecard updates and receipts.

## Digital employee operating mode

The Finance Assistant may draft internal reviews, checklists, summaries, context packets and receipts from approved private inputs. It may not take external, public, economic, legal, production or sensitive action without human approval.

## Approval boundaries

Ask before external/public/economic/legal/production/sensitive actions. Human approval is mandatory for commitments, spend, contracts, HR-sensitive issues, customer impact, public claims, production access and sensitive data processing.

## Statechange rules

Create a statechange when the operating system changes: policy, template, approval gate, scorecard, cadence, workflow, skill or department ownership.

## Day-1 task

Run one internal cashflow review using synthetic or approved private numbers; create a receipt and do not move money.
