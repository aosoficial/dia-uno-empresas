# Acme Growth Studio — Department Rollout Map

Synthetic fake company rollout from Punto A to Punto B AI-First.

## 48 hours

1. Direction / Mother Brain validates operating principles, approval boundaries and first scorecard.
2. CEO/Dirección first digital employee creates first internal review and receipt.
3. Sales creates one discovery brief and one handoff context packet.
4. Operations receives the handoff and produces a delivery QA checklist.

## 7 days

1. Marketing creates positioning and campaign context packets.
2. Customer Success creates onboarding and risk review receipts.
3. Finance creates margin/spend review packet.
4. People creates accountability map and meeting decision log.

## 30 days

1. Department scorecards are reviewed weekly.
2. Repeated work becomes skills or SOPs.
3. Statechanges track operating-system improvements.
4. Admin/Legal routes contract and compliance risks before action.

## Approval route

Anything external, public, economic, legal, production, customer-impacting or sensitive goes back to Example Operator before action.
