# Agency, Consultancy and Freelancer Vertical

The primary customer for DIA UNO Empresas is a service business becoming AI-First: an agency, consultancy or freelancer-led firm that wants productized delivery, operational memory and digital employees.

## Point A

- Knowledge scattered across calls, chats, docs and people.
- Delivery depends on founder memory.
- Processes are manual or undocumented.
- AI is used case-by-case, not embedded in operations.
- No brain per department.
- No integrated agents or memory feedback loop.
- No clear approval boundaries.

## Point B

- Productized service business with clear operating rhythm.
- Dirección / Mother Brain installed first.
- Department brains for delivery, marketing, sales, customer success, product/software, finance and people.
- Humans and digital employees work from the same operating context.
- Receipts, statechanges and context packets preserve evidence.
- Skills and templates improve from real work.
- Roadmap for 48h, 7 days and 30 days.

## Company type adaptations

### Agency

Focus: campaigns, creative, content, paid media, websites, growth and delivery quality.

Start with Dirección, Operations/Delivery, Marketing, Sales and Customer Success.

### Consultancy

Focus: diagnosis, proposals, expert delivery, workshops, client outcomes and knowledge capture.

Start with Dirección, Operations/Delivery, Sales, Customer Success and Knowledge/Productization.

### Freelancer

Focus: founder leverage, repeatable service package, proposals, follow-up, delivery SOPs and finance hygiene.

Start with Dirección, Operations/Delivery and Sales/Marketing combined until volume justifies separation.

## AI-First does not mean agent-only

Humans remain accountable. Agents help with memory, drafting, analysis, operations, follow-up and improvement. The system becomes AI-First when AI is embedded in the operating model, not when there are many bots.
