# 35 — Digital Employee Catalog

Select the first digital employee by the company's bottleneck, not by novelty. Every employee needs identity, owner, permissions, memory policy, tools and receipts before active work.

## Founder Assistant

- Use when: the founder is the bottleneck for decisions, prioritization and weekly review.
- Inputs: company context, scorecard, active blockers, decision log.
- Outputs: weekly review, decision drafts, context packets, follow-up list.
- Approval boundaries: cannot commit externally, spend, publish or change production without approval.
- First receipt: Dirección weekly review receipt.

## Sales Assistant

- Use when: leads and follow-ups are inconsistent.
- Inputs: ICP, offer, lead source, CRM fields, prior conversations.
- Outputs: lead qualification, next-step draft, pipeline hygiene report.
- Approval boundaries: cannot contact leads or change CRM-owned commercial facts without approval.
- First receipt: lead qualification receipt.

## Proposal Assistant

- Use when: proposals are slow or inconsistent.
- Inputs: approved offer, pricing guardrails, scope, client context.
- Outputs: proposal outline, assumptions, risks, approval checklist.
- Approval boundaries: cannot send proposals, promise dates, discount or sign.
- First receipt: proposal draft receipt.

## Delivery QA Assistant

- Use when: delivery quality depends on manual senior review.
- Inputs: scope, acceptance criteria, delivery artifacts, QA checklist.
- Outputs: QA findings, risk list, fix suggestions, evidence links.
- Approval boundaries: cannot change client-facing deliverables or production systems without approval.
- First receipt: delivery QA receipt.

## Client Success Assistant

- Use when: renewals, issues or client health are reactive.
- Inputs: client goals, delivery status, feedback, support issues, renewal dates.
- Outputs: health review, risk flags, renewal prep, issue summaries.
- Approval boundaries: cannot message clients, offer credits or change commitments without approval.
- First receipt: client health receipt.

## Finance Hygiene Assistant

- Use when: invoices, margin or cashflow are not reviewed weekly.
- Inputs: invoice list, payment status, expected costs, delivery hours.
- Outputs: finance hygiene review, missing invoice list, margin risk notes.
- Approval boundaries: cannot move money, send invoices or change terms without approval.
- First receipt: finance hygiene receipt.

## SOP Builder

- Use when: repeated work or corrections are not becoming process.
- Inputs: repeated task, examples, quality bar, owner correction.
- Outputs: SOP draft, skill candidate, validation checklist.
- Approval boundaries: cannot update public method or mandate process changes without approval.
- First receipt: SOP draft receipt.

## Meeting / Context Packet Assistant

- Use when: meetings create useful context but no operational memory.
- Inputs: notes/transcript, attendees, decisions, actions, risks.
- Outputs: context packet, action list, decision candidates, receipts needed.
- Approval boundaries: cannot store sensitive transcripts publicly or send summaries externally without approval.
- First receipt: context packet receipt.

## Selection rule

- If everything is chaotic: start with Founder Assistant.
- If revenue is blocked: Sales Assistant or Proposal Assistant.
- If delivery is risky: Delivery QA Assistant.
- If retention is weak: Client Success Assistant.
- If cash is unclear: Finance Hygiene Assistant.
- If knowledge repeats: SOP Builder.
- If meetings are the source of truth: Meeting / Context Packet Assistant.
