# Dirección / Cerebro Madre

Dirección is the first department in every Company Brain installation. It is the company-level brain that defines what the business is, who it serves, how it wins, who owns what, what agents may do, and what must stay human-approved.

## Why Dirección comes first

A service business should not add agents before it has a clear operating system. Dirección creates the source of truth for:

- mission, offer, ideal customer and positioning;
- strategic priorities and constraints;
- accountability seats before agent seats;
- approval boundaries for money, legal, production, external communication and sensitive data;
- department rollout order;
- operating cadence and scorecard;
- memory policy and evidence requirements.

## Inputs

- Company name, sector and company type: agency, consultancy or freelancer-led service business.
- Current Point A symptoms: scattered knowledge, manual processes, unclear owners, ad hoc AI use.
- Desired Point B outcomes: productized service, department brains, digital employees, feedback loop.
- Current tools: docs, CRM, project management, messaging, storage, analytics.
- Risk level: internal-only, client-visible, production, regulated or sensitive data.

## Dirección outputs

- `company/company-brain.md` — company-level memory and strategy.
- `departments/direction/department-brain.md` — Dirección brain.
- `company/accountability-map.md` — people seats before agent seats.
- `company/approval-boundaries.md` — limits and required approvals.
- `company/scorecard.md` — 5-15 weekly measurable signals.
- `company/operating-cadence.md` — weekly review, issue solving and receipts.
- `roadmap/48h-7d-30d.md` — rollout plan.
- `receipts/installation-receipt.md` — evidence of installation.

## Operating rule

Agents may draft, analyze, organize, propose and create internal receipts. They ask before external/public/economic/legal/production/sensitive actions.

## Dirección first 48h checklist

1. Capture company Point A in a context packet.
2. Define company type and primary service line.
3. Write company brain and approval boundaries.
4. Create accountability map with human owners.
5. Create department rollout order.
6. Launch the CEO Agent for Dirección.
7. Run one safe internal task and leave a receipt.
