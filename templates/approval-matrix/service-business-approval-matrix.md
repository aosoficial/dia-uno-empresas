# Service Business Approval Matrix

Use this matrix to decide what a digital employee can do without asking.

| Action | Draft | Execute internally | External/public/client | Economic/legal | Production/sensitive |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Summarize internal notes | allowed | allowed with source | approval | approval | approval |
| Draft proposal | allowed | approval to store | approval to send | approval | approval |
| Update CRM | allowed as suggestion | approval unless explicitly scoped | approval | approval | approval |
| Create SOP/skill draft | allowed | allowed in private draft | approval to publish | approval | approval |
| Finance review | allowed | allowed read-only | approval | approval | approval |
| Client communication | draft only | no | approval | approval | approval |
| Tool integration | plan only | approval | approval | approval | approval |

## Default rule

Drafting and analysis are safe by default. Sending, publishing, spending, legal commitment, production change, sensitive data movement and client-facing action require human approval.
