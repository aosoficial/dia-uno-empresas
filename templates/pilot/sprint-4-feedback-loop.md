# Sprint 4 — Feedback Loop

Outcome: corrections become better operating memory.

## Inputs

- digital employee output;
- human review;
- scorecard line;
- Receipt;
- observed blocker.

## Decide

- Was this a one-off correction?
- Should it update a department skill?
- Should it become a method improvement proposal?
- Is Approval required before changing the system?

## Done when

The correction is captured, the scorecard is updated and the next sprint is selected.
