# Sprint 2 — Department

Outcome: one department receives work through context packets and produces evidence.

## Create or review

- department brain;
- onboarding checklist;
- department skills;
- digital employee profile;
- handoff template.

## First workflow

Pick one workflow that is internal, reversible and reviewable.

## Evidence and Approval

Evidence must include the department brain, owner, cadence, inputs, outputs, first context packet and first Receipt. Approval is required before external/public/economic/legal/production/sensitive action.

Next action: run Sprint 3 only after the department owner accepts the workflow.

## Done when

The department has owner, cadence, inputs, outputs, approvals and a draft-only digital employee loop.
