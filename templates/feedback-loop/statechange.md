# Feedback Loop StateChange

Use this when the feedback loop changes the operating system itself.

## Change
What policy, skill, template, cadence, approval boundary, wizard output, scorecard, department brain or digital employee behavior changed.

## Why
Reference the human correction, change proposal and change contract. Explain why the old behavior was insufficient.

## New rule
State the new operational rule in plain language. Include allowed actions, forbidden actions and required approvals.

## Freshness
Current until replaced by a newer statechange.

## Impact
Departments, skills, digital employees, generated instances or validators affected.

## Validation
Tests, verifier output, safety scan and review evidence.

## Owner
`{{ owner }}` owns the decision and confirms whether this item may change the operating system.

## Source
`{{ source }}`. Include the exact human correction, observed failure, transcript excerpt, QA result, receipt or context packet that created the signal. Do not invent missing context.

## Approval
Human approval is required before external, public, economic, legal, production, customer-impacting or sensitive actions. If the change is safe and internal, record why it is safe.

## Evidence
Link the receipt, statechange, validation output, changed skill/template path and rollback note. Evidence must be sufficient for another operator to audit the change later.
