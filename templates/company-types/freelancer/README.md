# Freelancer Company Brain Profile

Use this profile for a freelancer becoming an AI-First productized service company.

## Focus

founder leverage, repeatable service package, proposals, follow-up, SOPs and finance hygiene.

## Recommended first departments

1. Dirección / Mother Brain
2. Operations / Delivery
3. Marketing
4. Sales
5. Customer Success / Postventa

For freelancers, Marketing and Sales can start combined until volume justifies separation.

## First 48h outcome

A private Company Brain instance with Dirección, approval boundaries, an accountability map, roadmap and one digital employee completing a safe internal task with receipt.
