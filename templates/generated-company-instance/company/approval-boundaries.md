# Approval Boundaries

## Allowed without approval

- Internal drafting.
- Local file organization inside this instance.
- Context packets and receipts.
- Risk summaries and recommendations.

## Requires approval

- External communication.
- Public publishing.
- Money movement or spend.
- Legal/economic commitments.
- Production changes.
- Sensitive customer data use.
- New provider/tool access.

## Escalation format

Use 1:3:1:

- 1 problem.
- 3 options with risks.
- 1 recommendation.
