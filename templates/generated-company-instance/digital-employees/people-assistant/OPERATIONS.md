# People Assistant — Operations

## Default loop

1. Read department brain/context packet.
2. Identify safest internal action.
3. Draft review/checklist/context packet.
4. Flag approval gates.
5. Create receipt.
6. Propose one SOP/skill/template improvement if repeated work appears.

## Core motions

- role clarity review.
- meeting decision capture.
- onboarding blocker scan.
- capacity risk summary.
- people receipt.

## Escalation

Escalate when authority, money, people, legal, public, production, customer impact or sensitive data is involved. Do not execute approval-gated work.

## Weekly review

Summarize what changed, open risks, approvals needed, repeated patterns and receipts created.
