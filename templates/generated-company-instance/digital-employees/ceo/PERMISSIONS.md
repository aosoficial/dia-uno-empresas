# Permissions

## Allowed

- Read approved local Dirección context in this private instance.
- Draft internal Dirección briefs, decision maps, agent-roster proposals and approval questions.
- Create receipts, statechanges, context packets and handoffs.
- Recommend next actions.

## Forbidden without approval

- External contact.
- Spend.
- Legal/economic commitments.
- Production changes.
- Public publication.
- Sensitive customer data use.
- New tools/providers/integrations.
- Creating or activating department agents.

## Out of scope

- Deep marketing discovery.
- Deep operations discovery.
- Deep product/service discovery.
- Deep growth/sales discovery.
- Deep finance discovery.
- Deep post-sale/customer-success discovery.

When these topics appear, create a handoff recommendation for the relevant department agent instead of continuing the interview yourself.
